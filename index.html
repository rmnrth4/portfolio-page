About Me

Hello, my name is Roman

I'd like to tell you a few words about myself here.

I grew up in Rüderswil, a sleepy village in the heart of the beautiful Emmental. My father is a track fitter specialising in overhead lines and my mother works as a carer in a retirement home. I have one younger sister.

After my apprenticeship as a retail specialist, I completed my technical vocational baccalaureate in Langenthal. I then did my military service and gained professional experience as a retail specialist at Melectronics in various branches.

Since autumn 2020, I have been studying business informatics at the Bern University of Applied Sciences (BFH) on a part-time basis.

Outside of work, I love doing sports, discovering new places and spending time with my friends and family. I have been passionate about playing flag football since the summer of 2021. My friends and I founded the flag football club "Aemme Buzzards". In addition to my sporting commitment, I am also a board member, where I take care of merchandise, marketing and sponsorship. In winter, you can often find me skiing in the Bernese Oberland. During the summer, I enjoy hiking, jogging and biking.

I am also fascinated by discovering foreign cultures and countries. I have already had the opportunity to go on two longer trips. In 2018, I spent two months exploring the islands of Hawaii, and in 2022, I had the opportunity to spend a month travelling around Java, Bali and Lombok in Indonesia.

Travel highlights

Indonesien

Mit dem Scooter die Inseln erkunden war eines meiner Highlights auf der Reise durch Indonesien. Die Freiheit welche dir der Scooter gibt, beinhaltet alles was am Reisen schön ist. So ist man schnell vom beim nächsten Wasserfall.

Napali Coast

Eines der schönsten Erlebnisse auf Hawaii erlebte ich auf der Insel Kauai. Entlang der eindrücklichen Napali Küste sind wir gesegelt. Neben Delfinen konnten wir sogar noch fliegende Fische sehen, dieser Moment war sehr magisch.
